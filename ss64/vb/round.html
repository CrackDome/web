Round

Round a number to n decimal places.

Syntax
       Round(Expression [, NumDecimalPlaces )])

Key
   Expression        A Numeric expression

   NumDecimalPlaces  Decimal places to keep
                     1=> 12.1
                     3=> 12.123

Example

myNum=123.4567
myNewNum=Round(myNum,2)
WScript.Echo myNewNum
